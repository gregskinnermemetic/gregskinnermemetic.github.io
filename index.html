Welcome to memetic

Transform your company by radically improving your execution.

what?

memetic is a consultancy that specialises in making companies better by improving delivery & execution.

We do this by using any paradigms that are relevant to what the business is trying to achieve: agile, lean, customer centricity, you name it.

Our approach is inspired by memetics: we beleive that great new execution practices should self-propagate through a culture. When it's done right, people will want to make it work for them.

how?

We believe that changing the way value gets delivered can be easy, more efficent, and fun.

In fact, that's how we think change can, and should, be memetic.

They are our guiding principles for any change effort to be successful:

1.

Easy

The new way of working must be easier to learn and execute than the way that it is replacing. This could mean less steps, better tooling, better education or a way to better understand why we're doing what we're doing in the first place.

2.

Efficient

Getting better results for less effort means people and businesses finish the day with more choices available to them, giving them flexibility in increasingly fast-paced and uncertain times

3.

Enjoyable

Better results happen when a workforce - from senior executives through to the freshest of new hires - find their work rewarding. Enjoyable can mean that we learn as we work, it can mean we're competitive, or it can just mean there's something plain fun about it.
