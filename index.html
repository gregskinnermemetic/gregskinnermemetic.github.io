A memetic approach

Successful change should be easy, efficent, and enjoyable.

That's how we arrived at our principles:

Easy

Any new way of working must be easier to learn and execute than the way that it is replacing.

Efficient

Getting better results for less effort means people and businesses finish the day with more choices available, giving flexibility in increasingly fast-paced and uncertain times.

Enjoyable

Better results happen when a workforce - from senior executives through to the freshest of new hires - find their work rewarding.
